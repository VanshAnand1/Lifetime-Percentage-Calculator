This website can be used to determine what percentage of your lifetime a time period has lasted. It takes in the start and end dates of the time period, as well as your birthdate. It calculates the amount of days the event lasted, and the number of days you have been alive, and returns the percentage. One use of this calculator is find out what percentage of your life you have known someone.
